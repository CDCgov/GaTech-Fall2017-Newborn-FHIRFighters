Since this application was written to satisfy a very specific need of Utah's newborn screening program, there was not much research needed.

We used the Utah newborn screening program's website to establish the initial requirements. 

http://health.utah.gov/newbornscreening/

Extensive use was made of the FHIR specifications to get the NBS records into a FHIR server

http://hl7.org/fhir/

Since birth weight and length are observations, we found the LOINC code search useful.

https://search.loinc.org/searchLOINC/search.zul

Finally, the problem of record linkage is harder than it looks.  Wikipedia has a good article on it.

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Record_linkage
